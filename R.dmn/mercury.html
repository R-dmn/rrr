The Swift Planet

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun.

Quick Facts

  • Diameter: 4,879 km
  • Moons: None
  • Orbit Period: 88 Earth days

Mercury's surface resembles that of Earth's Moon

Did You Know?

Mercury has a very thin atmosphere, causing extreme temperature variations.
