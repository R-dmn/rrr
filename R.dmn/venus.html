Earth's Twin

Venus is similar in size and structure to Earth but with a toxic atmosphere.

Quick Facts

  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Moons: None
  • Orbit Period: 225 Earth days

Venus has a dense atmosphere of carbon dioxide

Did You Know?

Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most planets.
